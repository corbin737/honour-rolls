Bakery Orders

Placing Your Order

To place an order, send us an email at order@honourrolls.ca with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email
  • Your phone number
  • The items you want to order AND the quantity of each item
  • Whether you would like to pick up the order or us to drop it off (see below)

Pick Up & Drop Off

Pick Up

We will contact you via the phone number and email you provide when your order is ready. Expect us to take between 2 and 4 business days depending on the size of your order.

After we contact you, we'll hold your order for a maximum of 3 business days.

See our contact page for our address and directions.

Drop Off

When you send us an email for your order, include an address for where and a time for when you would like the order dropped off. We only deliver between 7:00am and 7:00pm daily, and to local addresses.

We'll call you before the delivery time at the number provided above to confirm the drop off.
